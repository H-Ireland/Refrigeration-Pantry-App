OUR MISSION

QwikCooks is an app for those looking to make quality meals on a budget. Typically we target folks from lower to middle income homes, and provide "Qwik" and easy to make recipes. We understand that life is busy, so we provide recipes that take 30min or less to make. In 2013, Nutrition Journal published a study that reported that in 1965, between 88 and 95 percent of meals were prepared at home, whereas in 2007 between 65 and 72 percent were. That's a 16 to 30 percent drop. While we have steadily climbed back to an estimated 82%, that percentage may take a dive with apps like Uber Eats, Postmates and GrubHub making it easier to get food delivered.
